The Future

Building real in the AI age

The next generation is growing up in AI and crypto. The tools change overnight — the fundamentals that decide who wins don’t.

By Dyrique Mendoza, Founder & Principal · Email
4 min read · Jul 2026
In brief
  • The next generation will build in AI and crypto — the tools are moving faster than any generation has ever seen.
  • But the fundamentals don’t change: a real business, real revenue, and fundability still decide who gets funded.
  • AI is leverage, not a shortcut — and the crypto world runs the same old scams in new clothes. Build real.

The kids coming up won’t remember a world before AI. To most people that sounds like a threat. To a generation that’s native to it, it’s the biggest opening in history — as long as they build on solid ground.

The tools change. The fundamentals don’t.

AI can write your copy, design your brand, and crunch your numbers in seconds. But when you walk into a bank, the questions are the same ones from twenty years ago: Is this a real business? Does it make money? Can I verify it? AI doesn’t rewrite that test — it just helps you pass it faster.

AI is leverage, not a shortcut

Used right, AI lets one founder do the work of a team of ten. But the strategy, the taste, the relationships, and the ownership — that’s still you. The founders who win pair the machine’s speed with human judgment. The ones who let AI think for them build nothing worth owning.

The generation native to AI can be its customers — or its owners. The difference is whether you built something real.

Crypto and the new gold rush

Crypto and Web3 are real, and they’re not going anywhere. But every gold rush drags the same con artists along with it: “guaranteed 10x,” pump-and-dumps, rug pulls, “get in before it moons.” It’s the exact same trap that hit the credit-repair world — just wearing a hoodie and a ticker symbol. Free money with no risk is always a lie.

The rule still applies. Build a real, fundable business first — that’s your foundation. Anything speculative (a coin, a flip, a bet) is played with money you can afford to lose, never the thing holding you up. Own the upside; don’t get owned by the hype.

Own the future, don’t rent it

The move isn’t to chase the next AI tool or the next token. It’s to build a real business — legit, fundable, generating revenue — and use the new tools as leverage to move faster than any generation before you. That’s how you go from a user of the future to an owner of it.

You were born into the AI economy. Build something real inside it, and it’s yours.
