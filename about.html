Who We Are

Built for the ones they turn away

Dyrique Mendoza LLC is a fundability firm for the next generation — operating from Brickell, rooted in the community that raised its founder.

The giants on Brickell Avenue teach the Fortune 500 how to structure, fund, and protect their wealth. A fifteen-minute drive away, a generation with just as much drive was never handed the map. We exist to close that distance.

The firm

Dyrique Mendoza LLC is a Florida limited liability company operating from 1221 Brickell Avenue — the same financial district as the firms that price out the people we serve. We do one thing: make young and first-generation founders fundable, using the exact discipline the giants reserve for their biggest clients, in language anyone can understand.

Meet the founder

Dyrique Mendoza is a Miami native who learned this ladder the hard way and decided the next kid shouldn’t have to. He built this firm on a simple belief: the information was never the hard part — access was. So he’s giving it away, and standing beside the people who want a hand using it.

What we believe

That community is the point. That you should own, not just work. That doing it real — no gimmicks, no shortcuts that become someone else’s trap — is the only thing that lasts. And that a generation that looks like the future deserves to be built for it.

The mission

A kid building a place for kids. Young founders growing and thriving together, pulling each other up. That’s the movement — and everyone’s invited.
